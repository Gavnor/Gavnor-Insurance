Understanding Life Insurance

Life insurance provides financial security for your loved ones in the event of your passing. Here's a quick overview of its key types and benefits:

Types of Life Insurance

  • Term Life Insurance: Offers coverage for a set period at lower premiums.
  • Whole Life Insurance: Provides lifelong coverage and builds cash value over time.
  • Universal Life Insurance: Includes flexible premiums and investment components.
