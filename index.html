Why Life Insurance Matters

Life insurance isn't just a policy – it's peace of mind for your loved ones. Here’s why it’s essential:

  • Financial Security: Provides income replacement and debt coverage.
  • Legacy Planning: Helps support your family’s future education or inheritance.
  • Affordability: Easier to secure lower premiums when you're young and healthy.

Types of Life Insurance

  • Term Life: Coverage for a set number of years, ideal for temporary needs.
  • Whole Life: Permanent coverage with cash value accumulation.
  • Universal Life: Flexible premium payments and adjustable coverage.

How to Choose the Right Policy

  • Evaluate your family’s financial needs.
  • Compare policy benefits and premiums.
  • Talk to a licensed insurance advisor.
